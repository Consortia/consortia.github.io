About
Consortia is a creative office in New York City. We develop new frameworks for communication.

Our practice draws on research and thought leadership around lifestyle, technology, and cultural production in collaboration with a network of artists, designers, and other creative voices.

We envision and produce strategies for brand innovation that connect industry leaders with cultural and academic institutions along with independent thinkers.

Through new formats combining physical activations and digital content, our projects generate actionable knowledge and cultural impact.
